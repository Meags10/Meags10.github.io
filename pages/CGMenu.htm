Hot Café Drinks


Hot café drinks are served as a small (12oz) or a large (16oz). Sorry! No mediums here! All of our cold drinks are 16oz servings; go big or go home! For those of you who not keen on regular milk, we can make your drink with soy milk (add $0.60). Add one of our 18 flavor syrups to any drink for $0.74 extra. Sugar free syrups available!

Harborside Common Grounds

Harborside Common Grounds is a popular, locally owned coffee shop in downtown Kenosha, Wisconsin that boasts of a quaint, but energetic atmosphere. We offer a variety of traditional espresso drinks, as well as a few of our own creations that we've stumbled upon over the years. In addition to espresso drinks, we also brew specialty drip coffees, such as Butt Naked, Sugar Daddy, Drunken Uncle, and Vanilla Almond. For the none coffee drinkers and our younger customers, we serve Big Train® Chai, hot tea from The Republic of Tea®, and Ghirardelli® hot chocolate.

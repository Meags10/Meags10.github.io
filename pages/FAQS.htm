FAQ's

When does the deck open?

Typically, the deck is open at the beginning of May. However, due to Wisconsin's unpredictable weather patterns, this is not always possible. In addition, the deck will only be open on days when the weather permits.

Do you have WiFi?

Common Grounds is for those who would like to enjoy the company and comradey of friends and family over a cup of coffee. In an effort to maintain this atmosphere, we do not have WiFi.

What are your hours?

Like everyone else, your friendly baristas need a break, too! Monday - Saturday we are open from 7am to 9pm and on Sundays we're open from 7am to 7pm. Please note that we stop serving sandwiches 1 hour before close.

Where are you located?

We're located on the corner of 52nd Street and 6th Avenue in downtown Kenosha; We are also located walking distance from the train station, museums, and many other downtown attractions.

Are you currently hiring?

Like us on Facebook! We'll post on our page when we're looking for new baristas!

Do you sell ice cream?

Nope! But, if you walk down 6th Avenue just south of Common Grounds, there are two ice cream shops: Sandy's Popper and Scoop's Ice Cream.

Am I allowed to bring my pet on the deck?

As much as the staff would love to have dogs in the coffee shop, we can't allow pets on the deck or in the shop. However, your pet is more than welcome to join you at the picnic tables and benches in front of the shop. We also offer water dishes for our four legged friends.

Where does your art come from?

All of our art is from Lemon Street Gallery, a cooperative of local artists from the Kenosha area. They choose the pieces, we sell them. Pieces are rotated every three months and are also on display at Lemon Street Art Gallery on the corner of Sheridan Road and 46th Street in downtown Kenosha.

Can I sell my art here?

Unfortunately we only allow Lemon Street Gallery to hang their work at Common Grounds. If you are an artist who is interested in hanging artwork up at Common Grounds, please talk to Lemon Street Gallery.

When do you stop serving sandwiches?

Monday - Saturday we stop serving sandwiches at 8pm and on Sundays we stop serving sandwiches at 6pm. We stop serving breakfast sandwiches at 9:45am.

How do I find the soup of the day?

Like us on Facebook! We love posting creative statuses on our page about the soup of the day.
